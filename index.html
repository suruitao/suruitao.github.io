Welcome! I am currently working in the Computational Design and Fabrication Group at MIT. My research focuses on the digital design and additive manufacturing of active electronic systems and their applications in computational imaging and robotic sensing. During my doctoral research in the McAlpine Group, I investigated the methodologies of fabricating optoelectronic devices and microfluidic devices entirely on 3D printing platforms. Utilizing multi-modal printing methods, I demonstrated functional semiconducting devices and explored the process-structure-property space by leveraging the material viscoelasticity and yield-stress behavior, leading to 3D-structured photodetector matrices, OLED displays, and microfluidic systems.
